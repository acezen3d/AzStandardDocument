## Koikatsu Target Shader
- Koikano/main_eyew
- Shader Forge/toon_eyew_lod0

## Setup
- RenderType: Transparent
- Cull: [property]
- SrcBlend: SrcAlpha
- DstBlend: OneMinusSrcAlpha
- ZWrite: Off
- Keywords: _ALPHABLEND_ON
- Queue: Transparent-1
